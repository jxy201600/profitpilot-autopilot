# Judging Scorecard Strategy

Official scoring priorities are interpreted as:

| Category | Weight | ProfitPilot Evidence |
| --- | ---: | --- |
| Innovation & AI Creativity | 30% | Role-pipeline agent, compliance-first planning, quote/order/delivery workflow artifacts |
| Technical Depth & Engineering | 30% | Qwen Cloud adapter, deterministic fallback, structured JSON, CLI, web demo, tests, scorecard |
| Problem Value & Impact | 25% | Small-business revenue workflow, quote automation, payment gate, bilingual output |
| Presentation & Documentation | 15% | README, architecture, demo script, Devpost draft, blog post draft |

## Positioning

Do not pitch this as a generic chatbot. Pitch it as a business workflow autopilot that produces usable artifacts and enforces operational gates.

## Demo Must Show

1. A real inquiry.
2. Structured quote packet.
3. Payment confirmation gate.
4. Delivery preview.
5. Restricted request blocking.
6. Qwen Cloud live mode or clearly documented API integration.

## What To Avoid

- No gambling, crypto speculation, CBD/THC, exploit, or regulated advice workflows.
- No claim that payment is automatically confirmed without official merchant API.
- No third-party account auto-posting.
- No hidden credentials in repo or video.
